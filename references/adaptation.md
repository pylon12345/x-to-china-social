# Adaptation rules

## Fidelity layers

Separate three layers in the output:

1. What the original author stated
2. Necessary context or translation notes
3. The editor's interpretation or takeaway

Never blend layer 3 into layer 1. Preserve uncertainty, qualifications, numbers, product names, and links. Translate meaning naturally instead of mirroring English sentence order.

## 可见署名与来源边界

- 发布正文默认不点名原作者，不写“某作者分享/某作者认为”，也不设置单独的“援引作者”段。
- 使用“原文提出”“这套做法”的中性表达，避免把来源作者的经历转移给发布者。
- 文末保留 `原文：URL` 与 `摘要改写` 或 `翻译整理` 声明。不得同时删除原文链接和改编声明。
- 作者名与 handle 继续保存在 `source.json`、Obsidian frontmatter 和内部收据中，便于追溯，但不要求出现在公众号/小红书可见正文。

## Xiaohongshu

Lead with the reader benefit or surprising conclusion. Use short paragraphs and concrete subheads. Remove X-specific conversational debris only when it does not change meaning. Keep the body useful without clickbait or fabricated personal experience. End with `原文：URL` and disclose `翻译整理` or `摘要改写`.

## WeChat

Add enough introduction for readers who did not see the original discussion. Use a clear hierarchy and retain supporting reasoning. Put editor additions under labels such as `编者按` or `我的观察`. End with the canonical URL and an adaptation disclosure; omit the author callout from the visible article by default.

## Rights-sensitive content

For a short public post, quote only what is needed and add commentary/context. For long threads and X Articles, prefer a Chinese summary with selective short quotations. Keep original media attribution and do not download or republish media when rights are unclear.
